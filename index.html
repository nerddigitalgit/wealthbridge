The First Step to More Wealth

Stop losing 50 cents on every dollar.

The ultra-wealthy don't start by earning more. They start by keeping more. Before you can grow wealth, you have to stop the drains that are bleeding it away.

1

Find the Drains

Audit where your money is actually going. Taxes, structure, cash flow, interest — identify every dollar leaving that doesn't have to.

2

Fix Your Structure

Incorporation is layer one. But are you structured for wealth building or just day-to-day operations? Set up the right entities for where you're going.

3

Eliminate Unnecessary Tax

Your biggest expense isn't your mortgage — it's your taxes. Use corporate-owned insurance, capital dividend accounts, and proper compensation to reduce tax by 30-40%.

4

Activate Retained Earnings

Money sitting in your corporation should be working. Position it in tax-exempt vehicles that grow 5-6% annually — accessible without triggering income.

5

Create Liquidity Without Tax

Need cash? Borrow against assets instead of withdrawing income. Pay 5% interest instead of 36-50% tax. The money keeps growing. You get access.

6

Design Tax-Free Retirement

The goal: multiple streams of tax-free income in retirement. Design your exit strategy now so you keep what you earned — not half of it.
